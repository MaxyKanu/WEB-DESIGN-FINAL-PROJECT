Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide our journey in transforming Sierra Leone football

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and position Sierra Leonean football talent for successful professional careers through comprehensive management and strategic international partnerships.

  • Talent identification nationwide
  • Professional development programs
  • International exposure opportunities
  • Career-long support and guidance

Our Vision

To become West Africa's leading football agency, transforming raw talent into world-class professionals and elevating Sierra Leone's presence in global football.

  • Regional football hub establishment
  • Youth academy development
  • International scouting network
  • Football infrastructure investment

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Community Development guide every decision we make in building sustainable football careers.

  • Ethical player representation
  • Transparent operations
  • Community engagement
  • Sustainable development
Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

From humble beginnings to becoming Sierra Leone's premier football agency

2015

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to professionalize football talent management in Sierra Leone. Started operations from a small office in Freetown with our first 5 players.

2017

First International Transfer

Successfully negotiated our first international transfer, moving a promising young talent to a European academy. This marked our entry into international football.

2019

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations across West Africa, establishing scouting networks in neighboring countries and forming partnerships with 5 additional clubs.

2021

Youth Academy Launch

Launched the "Future Stars" youth development program, focusing on identifying and nurturing talent from grassroots level across Sierra Leone.

2023

Digital Transformation

Implemented advanced scouting technology and launched our digital platform, revolutionizing how we identify and track talent across Africa.
