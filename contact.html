Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common inquiries about contacting us

How long does it take to get a response?
We respond to all inquiries within 2 business hours during working days. Emergency inquiries are prioritized and typically receive responses within 30 minutes.
What information should I include in my player inquiry?
Include your age, position, recent statistics, video highlights, and contact information. The more detailed your profile, the faster we can assess opportunities for you.
Do you charge fees for player representation?
No upfront fees. We work on a success-based commission model, only earning when you succeed. Our priority is developing your career, not collecting fees.
Can clubs from outside Sierra Leone contact you?
Absolutely! We work with clubs worldwide. Our extensive network across Africa allows us to source talent for international clubs looking for African players.
What's your emergency contact procedure?
For urgent matters (injuries, contract emergencies, etc.), call our emergency line at +232 78 801 801. We have staff on call 24/7 for critical situations.
